Learner Admission

Admission for new learners for Grade 7 & Grade 8 at Qholaqhwe Intermediate School for 2022.

Parents are urged to register their children for admission between 08 August 2021 and 30 September 2021, Time: 12pm to 14pm


The following documents are required for registration:

  • Birth Certificate of a learner
  • ID Copy of Parents
  • Proof of Residance
  • Provincial transfer
  • Term 1 Report
