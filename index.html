Qholaqhwe Intermediate School

School Motto

Rise, Shine and Reach for the Stars

The school provide quality and sustainable education to learners who are between the age of 12 and 16 years old from the community of Makwane Areas: (Lusaka, Qholaqhwe village, Tebang village, Matsikeng village and Khabisi).

Mission

  • To encourage parental and community involvement in our school.
  • To provide quality education by means of institution, guidance and facilitation by a core of quality and accountable educators.
  • To establish a well-equiped school environment that will cater for holistical needs of learners.
