Background

The school was established in 1985 by the Free State department of education and it situated at Qholaqhwe village in Makwane which is a rural area within a disadvantage communities of Lusaka, Qholaqhwe village, Tebang village, Matsikeng village and Khabisi.

under the leadership of Mr Mapeka K was the first principal of the school from 1985 up until September 2005, followed by Mr Mosethe M from October 2005 up until March 2014 then since from April 2014 the Acting principal was Mr Morajane D.S and Mrs Sekonyela M up until August 2017, then in 2017 September Mr Tsotetsi J.T became a new Principal of the school and he is still the present principal.

Our feeder schools are ZR Mahabane P/S, Lerato P/S, Kgoledi Ya Manka P/S, Matsikeng P/S and Boitelo P/S, because we are only Intermediate school in this area.
