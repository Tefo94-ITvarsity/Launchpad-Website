Entertainment

The school usually celebrades the following in each and every year: Valentine's Day, Spring day, Fare-well for Teaching and Non-Teaching staff and Harritage day.



Valantine's and Spring Day

Learners wear casual clothes and they contest at school.


Heritage Day

The school celebrates heritage day before the holiday, learners wear traditional clothing and they do mokgibo, ditolobonya, they sing traditional songs according to their cultures, they do drama and School Choir Sing traditional songs.



Educator's and Non-Teaching staff Fare-well

On the third week of the term3, the school celebrates fare-well for educatos and non teaching staff who have retired or who got the promotions to another schools.
